# Project Horizons — The Ascension System

**The Second Awakening.**

> Late-game reclass mechanic. Players who've mastered one path can transcend it — keeping everything they've earned while unlocking a new tree of power. Solo Leveling–style awakening, Path of Exile–style ascendancy, woven into the existing perk trees, the quest system, the endings, and the Watchers' lore.

---

## 1. The Concept

Around Act 4, the player has poured 100+ hours into one or more of the four base perk trees. They've cleared Gates, befriended Cobblemon, built farms, traded with kingdoms. They feel powerful — but the world hints at something more.

The Watcher offers a question: *Are you ready to become more than what you are?*

**Ascension** is the answer. The player undergoes a trial, transcends their current form, and unlocks an **Ascended Class** — a new identity that builds on what they were before, but reaches further.

### Three Rules

1. **You never lose what you earned.** Old perks, items, levels, Cobblemon, reputation — everything stays. Ascension is *additive*, not a reset.
2. **Ascension is earned, not bought.** No coin price will unlock it. You demonstrate mastery, complete a unique trial, and pass a Watcher's judgment.
3. **There is more than one ascension.** Players who complete the game can ascend a second and even third time, each more demanding than the last.

---

## 2. The Four Base Trees (Recap)

The existing perk system has four trees, each capping at 100 points. Quick recap:

- **🗡️ Vanguard** — Combat, dungeons, Cobblemon battles, weapons, dodge, magic
- **⚙️ Artificer** — Create, IE, automation, engineering, brewing tech, rocket science
- **🌾 Cultivator** — Farming, brewing, food, MineColonies, Cobblemon partnership
- **🧭 Wayfinder** — Exploration, mapping, fast travel, planet survey, lore discovery

A typical player will focus on 1-2 trees primarily, with light dabbling in others. **Ascension is about taking what you focused on and pushing it past its mortal limit.**

---

## 3. Ascension Trigger

You become eligible for Ascension when **all** of the following are true:

| Requirement | Why |
|---|---|
| Reached **Act 4** in the main quest | Lore — the Watchers must have revealed themselves |
| At least **one perk tree at 50+ points** OR **two trees at 25+ each** | Demonstrates mastery |
| Completed at least **one side chain** to its capstone | Demonstrates dedication beyond the main path |
| Cleared **Gate Floor 30** | Demonstrates combat or stealth competence |
| Earned **Trusted reputation** with at least one faction | Demonstrates social weight |
| Crime Stat **0 OR 4+** | You must be either fully lawful or fully outlaw — no middle ground for the Watchers |

When all conditions are met, **The Watcher's Voice** appears in dialogue at the Sky Cathedral, offering the **Trial of Ascension**.

## 4. The Ten Ascended Paths

Ten Ascended Classes total. Six are **Hybrids** (require investment in two base trees). Four are **Transcendents** (require maxing one base tree to its 100-point capstone).

Players choose **one path per Ascension**. Multiple Ascensions allow stacking different paths.

### Hybrid Ascended (require 50+ in two specific trees)

| # | Class | Combines | Identity |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Warlord** | Vanguard + Artificer | Mechanical war machine. Create-powered weapons, IE-forged armor, dungeon-clearing combat tank |
| 2 | **Beastmaster** | Vanguard + Cultivator | Cobblemon battle god. Permanent buffs to all owned Pokémon, special battle abilities, deep Trust unlocked |
| 3 | **Pathwalker** | Vanguard + Wayfinder | Dimensional assassin. Shadow-step combat, dungeon specialist, can phase between Gate floors |
| 4 | **Architect** | Artificer + Cultivator | Automation-farming overlord. Create + greenhouse fusion, MineColonies superpowers, food-as-fuel mastery |
| 5 | **Voyager** | Artificer + Wayfinder | Space pioneer. Builds rocket-tier gear, plants flags on every planet, mechanical exploration powers |
| 6 | **Shepherd** | Cultivator + Wayfinder | Wandering herbalist. Travel with portable farms, lead Cobblemon migrations, peaceful exploration mastery |

### Transcendent Ascended (require 100/100 capstone in one tree)

| # | Class | From | Identity |
|---|---|---|---|
| 7 | **Avatar of War** | Vanguard 100 | Pure combat god. Gate Floor 100 in one descent, legendary weapons, the bosses fear you |
| 8 | **Mind of the Forge** | Artificer 100 | Pure crafting god. Bend Create and IE to your will, recipes others cannot dream of |
| 9 | **Verdant Sovereign** | Cultivator 100 | Pure life god. Soil bows to you, Cobblemon flock to you, the world grows where you walk |
| 10 | **Eternal Walker** | Wayfinder 100 | Pure exploration god. The map opens, dimensions unfold, distance loses meaning |

## 5. What Ascension Grants

Each Ascended Class unlocks four things at the moment of ascension:

### A. A New 50-Point Perk Subtree

Exclusive to that class. Cannot be accessed any other way. Each subtree has 5 tiers of 10 perks. Capstone perks are class-defining abilities.

### B. A Passive Transformation

A permanent visible change to the player. Cosmetic + minor passive buff.

### C. A Signature Ability

A keybind-activated power unique to the class. Cooldown-based, dramatic.

### D. A Class Sigil

A Curio item that identifies the player as Ascended. Provides a small permanent stat bonus and shows the class title in chat.

## 6. The Ten Ascended Paths — Detailed

### 6.1 Warlord *(Vanguard + Artificer)*

**Identity:** A mechanical warlord who fuses metal to flesh, fights with steam-powered weapons, and treats dungeons like factories.

**New Subtree Highlights:**

- **Steam Fury** — combat strikes generate Create SU on hit
- **Iron Plate Soul** — IE-tier passive armor bonus
- **Forge-Blessed Edge** — your weapons can be tempered at Create stations for permanent damage bonuses
- **Mechanized Strike** — basic attacks chain into a 5-hit combo when at full SU
- **Capstone: Engine of Wrath** — for 30 seconds, every kill restores half your HP and resets weapon cooldowns

**Passive Transformation:** Brass-plated arms (visible cosmetic), +2 hearts permanent

**Signature Ability:** **Detonate** — slam your weapon into the ground, creating a Create-powered shockwave (10-block radius, knocks back enemies, breaks weak walls in dungeons). 60s cooldown.

**Class Sigil:** Iron Crown of Wrath — +5% damage, +10% physical resistance

---

### 6.2 Beastmaster *(Vanguard + Cultivator)*

**Identity:** A trainer who has bonded so deeply with their Pokémon that they fight as one. The peak of the Cobblemon path.

**New Subtree Highlights:**

- **Resonant Bond** — your active Pokémon's stats add 10% to your own in combat
- **Pack Tactics** — you can summon 2 Pokémon at once instead of 1
- **Shared Fate** — when you take fatal damage, your Soulbound Pokémon takes it instead (once per day)
- **Trainer's Roar** — battle cry buffs all your Pokémon for 60 seconds
- **Capstone: One With the Wild** — all your Pokémon gain a permanent +1 IV in every stat, and Soulbound Pokémon can use moves outside of battle

**Passive Transformation:** Subtle aura that matches your favorite Pokémon's type (visible)

**Signature Ability:** **Summit Call** — summon all 6 of your Pokémon at once for 30 seconds; they fight alongside you with full AI. 5-minute cooldown.

**Class Sigil:** Bonded Crystal — your Pokémon level up 25% faster

### 6.3 Pathwalker *(Vanguard + Wayfinder)*

**Identity:** A shadow-stepping assassin who treats Gate dungeons like a hunting ground and the world like a map of opportunities.

**New Subtree Highlights:**

- **Phase Step** — short-range teleport on dodge roll (3-block jump)
- **Floor Sense** — see Gate floor layout before descending
- **Ghost Strike** — backstabs deal 3x damage
- **Threadwalker** — fall damage immunity, plus brief slow-fall after high jumps
- **Capstone: Between Spaces** — you can phase between Gate floors at will, paying Pathfinder XP per phase

**Passive Transformation:** Faint shadow trails when sprinting (visible)

**Signature Ability:** **Shadow Cross** — teleport to any visible enemy within 30 blocks, dealing massive damage on arrival. 45s cooldown.

**Class Sigil:** Dim Anchor — +20% movement speed, -20% fall damage

- **Capstone: World-Shaper** — you can designate a 64x64 region as a "Tended Land" where crops grow 5x faster, soil never depletes, and Cobblemon spawn freely

**Passive Transformation:** Vines and small flowers grow on your boots when you stand still (cosmetic)

**Signature Ability:** **Bloom** — instantly mature all crops in a 16-block radius and restore them to fresh soil. 5-minute cooldown.

**Class Sigil:** Ringseed — you generate 1 random seed every 5 minutes, automatically

## 7. The Trials of Ascension

Each of the 10 Ascended Classes has a **unique trial dungeon** the player must clear to ascend. Trials are hand-built (not procedural Gates), themed to the class, and designed to test the player's mastery of that path.

### Trial Structure

- **Location:** A custom dimension or hidden Gate floor accessed via the Watcher's invitation
- **Difficulty:** Tuned for endgame players with full Tier 5+ gear
- **Length:** 30-90 minutes per trial
- **No respawn:** If you die, you exit the trial and must wait 24 game hours to retry
- **No party:** Trials are solo only (you and your Cobblemon companions)
- **Reward:** The Ascension itself, plus an exclusive trial-only loot drop

### Trial Themes

| Class | Trial Name | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| Warlord | **The Iron Crucible** | Fight wave after wave of mechanized constructs in a Create-powered colosseum |
| Beastmaster | **The Bond of Eight** | Win 8 consecutive Cobblemon battles against increasingly difficult NPC trainers |
| Pathwalker | **The Mirror Maze** | Stealth-puzzle dungeon — get caught and the trial restarts |
| Architect | **The Endless Field** | Build a fully self-sustaining farm in 30 minutes from scratch using only provided materials |
| Voyager | **The Long Journey** | Visit all 7 planets in one continuous trial, no return to spawn |
| Shepherd | **The Caravan's Tale** | Lead 5 wandering NPCs safely from one corner of a dangerous dimension to the other |
| Avatar of War | **The Hundred Floors** | Reach Gate Floor 100 in a single descent — no respawn anchors active |
| Mind of the Forge | **The Recipe of Worlds** | Solve a complex multi-step Create recipe puzzle without instructions |
| Verdant Sovereign | **The First Garden Trial** | Restore a dying ancient garden by reviving 100 plants in 30 minutes |
| Eternal Walker | **The Edge of the Map** | Find and reach a hidden Precursor monument at the literal edge of Aetheria's worldgen |

## 8. Multiple Ascensions

A character can ascend up to **three times**. Each subsequent ascension is harder and unlocks deeper content.

### First Ascension

- Requirements as listed in Section 3
- Choose any of the 10 Ascended Classes you qualify for
- Costs: 1 Bronze Ascension Crystal (granted by completing the trial)

### Second Ascension

- Must have completed at least **one game ending** (any of the 5)
- Must have invested **25+ points** in the new Ascended Class subtree
- Trial difficulty doubles
- Choose a different Ascended Class
- Costs: 1 Silver Ascension Crystal

### Third Ascension

- Must have unlocked the **Walker of All Paths** title (completed all 5 endings)
- Must have invested **50+ points** in two prior Ascended Class subtrees
- Trial difficulty triples — only the most prepared survive
- Choose any remaining class (cannot repeat)
- Costs: 1 Gold Ascension Crystal
- **Bonus:** Receive the **Triple Crown** Curio — a unique item showing all three ascension symbols

### What Stacking Ascensions Means

A triple-ascended character has:

- All four base perk trees available (with whatever points they invested)
- Three Ascended Class subtrees (50 points each = up to 150 additional perk points)
- Three Class Sigils (Curios)
- Three Signature Abilities (each with its own keybind)
- Three Passive Transformations (visible stacking aura)

This is an **endgame god build** — but it requires hundreds of hours of investment to achieve. Most players will Ascend once and call it a triumph.

## 9. Lore Integration

Ascension is not arbitrary. It's woven into the Precursor mythology and the Watchers' story.

### What Ascension Actually Is

The Precursors didn't just vanish — they **ascended**. The civilization that built Aetheria reached a point where their understanding of the world allowed them to transcend their physical forms. The Watchers are their fragments, their memories, their final witnesses.

When a player ascends, they're walking the same path the Precursors walked. They're proving themselves worthy of the legacy. The Watchers are watching to see who will follow — and who will fall.

### The Watcher's Blessing

Each Ascended Class is associated with one of the **Seven Watchers**, ancient figures who appear in lore throughout the main quest:

| Watcher | Domain | Ascended Classes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Watcher of Iron** | War, forge, defense | Warlord, Avatar of War |
| **The Watcher of Bonds** | Love, friendship, animals | Beastmaster |
| **The Watcher of Shadow** | Stealth, secrets, dimensions | Pathwalker |
| **The Watcher of Soil** | Growth, life, peace | Architect, Verdant Sovereign |
| **The Watcher of Stars** | Distance, light, navigation | Voyager, Eternal Walker |
| **The Watcher of Roads** | Travel, song, gathering | Shepherd |
| **The Watcher of Knowledge** | Craft, invention, understanding | Mind of the Forge |

When a player ascends, they appear briefly in the dream of their patron Watcher. The dream is shown via Custom NPC cutscene + custom dialogue — a brief, beautiful moment where the player meets a being who has been watching them since Act 1.

### Lore Books

Each Ascended Class unlocks a **Lore Codex** — a custom book item that contains:

- The Watcher's story
- Three historical Precursors who walked this path
- The class's signature techniques described as ancient martial arts / crafting traditions / philosophical schools
- A hint about the secret content tied to that class (see Section 12)

Lorekeepers (S5 side chain) gain bonus XP for collecting all 10 Codices.

## 10. Quest System Integration

Ascension is unlocked through the existing FTB Quests system, with a new chapter added to the quest book.

### New Quest Chapter: A6 — The Path of Ascension

Hidden until the player meets all Section 3 requirements. When the requirements are met, a new quest line appears in their quest book, and Sera (returning) tells them: *"There's someone waiting for you at the Sky Cathedral."*

#### Quest Line Structure

| # | Title | Giver | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| AS.01 | **The Watcher's Voice** | The Watcher (Sky Cathedral) | Speak with the Watcher and accept the trial offer |
| AS.02 | **Choose Your Path** ⚡ | The Watcher | Select the Ascended Class you wish to pursue (branching point) |
| AS.03 | **Prepare for the Trial** | The Watcher | Gather class-specific materials (varies per class) |
| AS.04 | **Enter the Trial** | Trial Door (location-based) | Enter the Trial dimension for your chosen class |
| AS.05 | **Survive the Trial** | The Trial itself | Complete the trial without dying |
| AS.06 | **The Ascension** | The Watcher (returning) | Return and undergo the ascension ritual |
| AS.07 | **First Steps Beyond** | Sera | Use your new powers in the world for the first time |

After completing AS.07, the new Ascended Class subtree opens, and the player can begin investing perks.

### Multiple Ascension Sub-Chapters

When the player completes their first ascension and meets the requirements for a second, **a second sub-chapter** opens (AS.08 through AS.14, themed for the second ascension).

The third ascension opens an even deeper sub-chapter (AS.15 through AS.21) that includes lore-only quests revealing the final truth about the Precursor ascension.

## 11. Crime System & Outlaw Variants

Outlaws don't get the same treatment as lawful players. Their Ascended Classes are different — darker, more dangerous, more powerful in different ways.

### The Shadow Path

If a player attempts the Trial of Ascension at Crime Stat 4+, the Watcher does not appear. Instead, they meet **Whisper** (Outlaw side chain hub NPC), who offers them an alternative:

> *"The Watchers won't bless someone like you. But the Forgotten will. Walk with me."*

The Forgotten are former Precursors who fell from grace — outcasts, criminals, the ones the Watchers refused. They offer **Shadow Variants** of the 10 Ascended Classes.

### Shadow Variants

Each lawful Ascended Class has a Shadow Variant. Same general theme, different mechanics, all leaning into criminal/outlaw playstyle.

| Lawful Class | Shadow Variant | Difference |
|---|---|---|
| Warlord | **Shadow Reaver** | Bonus damage to NPCs and hunters; signature ability spawns intimidation aura that reduces witness chance |
| Beastmaster | **Wild Hunter** | Cobblemon are trained for ambush; signature ability reduces enemy combat AI |
| Pathwalker | **Nightcloak** | Permanent invisibility while crouching; capture immunity for 60s after kill |
| Architect | **Smuggler-King** | Hidden caches that don't show on faction maps; counterfeit currency crafting |
| Voyager | **Void Pirate** | Can attack other players' Stellaris bases without crime escalation |
| Shepherd | **Wandering Outlaw** | Camps act as Outlaw Hideouts that grant safe haven |
| Avatar of War | **Tyrant** | All combat damage doubled; mass intimidation aura; NPC bounty hunters fear approaching you |
| Mind of the Forge | **Lockbreaker** | Can pick any kingdom lock; sabotage Create machines without detection |
| Verdant Sovereign | **Witch of Thorns** | Crops grow poisonous; can brew lethal toxins instead of wines |
| Eternal Walker | **Ghost of the Roads** | Cannot be tracked by Hunter's Compass; counter-bounties yield triple |

### Reputation Cost

Shadow ascensions come with a permanent cost:

- Crime Stat is **locked at 5** until the player completes a "Redemption Quest" (which costs every Shadow Variant perk earned)
- All friendly faction reputation is **permanently capped at Hostile**
- The Free Wanderers faction reputation is **permanently maxed at Honored**
- NPC Bounty Hunters spawn at **double rate**

This is the **risk/reward extreme** — Shadow players are immensely powerful but the entire world is their enemy. Some players will love this. Most will not.

## 15. Why This Works

Ascension serves several design goals at once:

**Endgame retention.** Players who hit 100 hours and feel "done" with progression have a clear next mountain to climb. Three ascensions means hundreds more hours of meaningful goals.

**Build identity.** Players go from "I'm a Vanguard with some Cultivator points" to "I'm a Beastmaster who walks with a Soulbound team and battles legends." Their identity becomes legible to other players.

**Lore integration.** The Watchers were already in the main quest. Ascension makes them *meaningful* — they've been watching from Act 1, and now they call you to your destiny. Players retroactively understand the early game better.

**Crime system depth.** Shadow Variants give the Outlaw path real endgame teeth. Without ascension, outlaws had nowhere to grow into. Now they have a parallel power ceiling that's just as deep.

**Replay value.** With 10 lawful + 10 shadow + 3 ascensions per character + 5 endings, there are *thousands* of distinct character builds. No two players will play the same.

**The fantasy.** Solo Leveling. Path of Exile ascendancy. Diablo 2 transformation. Final Fantasy job mastery. This is the moment players have wanted from RPGs forever, in Minecraft, with their own Cobblemon, in their own kingdom. **It's the payoff for everything else.**
